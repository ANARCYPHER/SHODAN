FAQ

How do I add a snippet?

If you're a member and logged-in to Shodan then you will see a big red "Add Command" button in the top left. At the moment, only members are able to add new snippets.

Why no multiline/ longer snippets?

The focus is on sharing short and useful commands. There are lots of other websites where you can find longer scripts to do various tasks.

Are all snippets public?

Yes! If you share a snippet then it will be publicly available to everybody.
